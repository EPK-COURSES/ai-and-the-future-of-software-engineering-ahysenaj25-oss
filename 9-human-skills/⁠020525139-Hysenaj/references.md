# References: Human Skills in the AI Era

Here are the academic and industry sources used to analyze the evolving role of software engineers alongside artificial intelligence:

1. **Association for Computing Machinery (ACM)**  
   *Title:* The Future of Computing Education: Balancing Technical Depth with Socio-Ethical Skills.  
   *Context:* Discusses why software engineers need critical thinking, ethics, and communication to oversee AI tools.

2. **IEEE Software Magazine**  
   *Title:* Software Engineering in the Age of Large Language Models.  
   *Context:* Analyzes how AI tools like Copilot change coding speeds, but increase the need for human system architects.

3. **Harvard Business Review**  
   *Title:* The Skills Leadership Demands When AI Does the Coding.  
   *Context:* Highlights the growing importance of emotional intelligence (EQ), requirements negotiation, and team management in tech teams.
